Anatomy of an Encounter

Each patient visit, lasting 15 to 45 minutes, follows a core documentation cycle, assisted by AI and verified by the scribe.

Idle Time Task Priority

During gaps between patients, scribes pivot to administrative tasks in a specific order to maximize productivity.

Communication Protocols

Confidential (PHI)

All communication containing patient information MUST use secure **athena Text**.

General & Non-Confidential

Use **WhatsApp** for attendance and group updates. Use **Google Meet** for live encounter communication.
